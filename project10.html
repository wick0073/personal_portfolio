Project LioRide

Developed Project LioRide, an innovative electric scooter integrated with a companion mobile application that enhances user control and experience.

The electric scooter was engineered with cutting-edge technology, allowing users to remotely power the scooter on and off via the app, monitor battery levels in real-time, and track the distance traveled. The mobile app, designed with a user-friendly interface, provides seamless connectivity with the scooter, offering features such as speed control, route tracking, and performance analytics. The project combined expertise in hardware design and software development to create a cohesive and intelligent transportation solution, demonstrating advancements in personal mobility and IoT integration. LioRide represents a forward-thinking approach to sustainable urban transport, with a focus on convenience, efficiency, and innovation.
