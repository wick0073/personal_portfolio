Web based system for a Machine hiring Company

❮ × ❯
Designed and developed a comprehensive web-based system tailored for a machine hiring company.

This system was built to streamline the management of equipment rentals, inventory tracking, and customer interactions. The platform featured an intuitive interface that allowed users to easily browse available machinery, request rentals, and track usage. Additionally, the system included robust backend functionalities for managing bookings, scheduling maintenance, and generating detailed reports on equipment utilization. The solution was designed to enhance operational efficiency, improve customer satisfaction, and support the company’s growth by automating key business processes.
