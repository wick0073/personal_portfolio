Business directory-Sri Lanka 2015

Conceptualized, designed, and developed a comprehensive business directory specifically for the Sri Lankan market in 2015.

The directory provided a centralized platform for users to easily search and access detailed information about businesses across various industries throughout the country.
This project involved the development of a user-friendly interface, advanced search functionalities, and an organized categorization system to enhance the user experience. The directory was also equipped with features such as location-based search, contact details, and business reviews, making it an essential tool for both consumers and businesses looking to connect within the Sri Lankan business landscape.
