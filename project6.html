Web based system for a Investment Company

❮ × ❯
Conceptualized, designed, and developed a robust web-based system in 2016 to efficiently manage and handle client relationships for an investment company.

The system was built to streamline various client management processes, including client onboarding, portfolio management, transaction tracking, and communication. It featured a secure and user-friendly interface that allowed investment advisors to easily access client information, generate reports, and provide personalized investment advice. Additionally, the system was designed with scalability in mind, ensuring it could accommodate the growing client base and evolving needs of the company, while maintaining high standards of data security and compliance with industry regulations.
